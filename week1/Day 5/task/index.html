Domestic animals are animals that live with humans and are often kept for companionship, work, or food.They are tamed and rely on humans for shelter, food, and care.

The cow is a gentle and useful domestic animal that has been a part of human life for centuries. It is a herbivore, feeding mainly on grass, hay, and other plant materials. Cows are valued for their milk, which is rich in nutrients and used to make products like butter, cheese, and yogurt.
